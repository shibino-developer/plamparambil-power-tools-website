Our Story

Plamparambil Trading was established in 2022 with a vision to provide high-quality power tools and spare parts to professionals and DIY enthusiasts alike. Despite being a relatively new entrant in the market, our foundation is built on decades of experience and expertise.

Our owner, who oversees the daily operations, brings over 30 years of experience from NPCC (National Petroleum Construction Company). His extensive background in mechanics, tools, and spare parts ensures that Plamparambil Trading is well-equipped to meet the diverse needs of our customers. His deep knowledge and passion for the industry drive our commitment to offering only the best products and services.

At Plamparambil Trading, we pride ourselves on our wide range of products, all subjected to rigorous quality assurance processes. We understand the importance of reliable tools and spare parts in both professional and personal projects, which is why we are dedicated to providing top-notch products that you can depend on.

Our team is always ready to provide expert advice and support, helping you choose the right tools for your specific needs. We also strive to offer competitive pricing, ensuring that you get the best value without compromising on quality. Customer satisfaction is at the heart of everything we do, and we are committed to making your experience with us as seamless and satisfying as possible.

We look forward to serving you and being your trusted partner for all your power tool and spare part needs.
